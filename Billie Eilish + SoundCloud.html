How her tracks went from bedroom to Billboard.

See how it all started, and how SoundCloud helped catapult her career:

2015
'Ocean Eyes' changes it all.

13- year-old Billie Eilish and her brother Finneas record 'Ocean Eyes' in their bedroom and release it on SoundCloud. Within hours, the single had 10,000 plays.

Listen
2016
Her second release, 'Six Feet Under,' goes viral.

Billie’s second single 'Six Feet Under' immediately captivates listeners, who can’t get enough of her haunting vocals and irreverent, genre-bending style.

Through SoundCloud they connect with fans directly through comments, reposts, and collaborations, building a loyal following, and industry insiders and media outlets start to take notice.

Listen
2017
‘dont smile at me’ reaches 14 on the Billboard Charts.

Billie signs with Interscope Records and releases her debut EP, 'dont smile at me,' which reaches 14 on the Billboard charts, transforming her from independent artist to global sensation.

Listen
2017
Later that year, she releases 'bitches broken hearts' on SoundCloud.

2019
‘WHEN WE ALL FALL ASLEEP, WHERE DO WE GO?’ hits #1 on the Billboard Charts, and so much more.

Billie hits #1 for the first time, and wins Best New Artist at the MTV Music Awards, New Artist of the Year at the American Music Awards, and Top New Artist at the Billboard Music Awards. She also sets the record for youngest female artist ever to top the UK album charts.
